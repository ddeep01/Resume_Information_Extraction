Research Interests:
My areas of interest have expanded over time.
I started my research journey with Statistical
Thermodynamics, Soft Condensed Matter, Molecular Dynamics (MD), and then I moved toward
Quantum Thermodynamics, Open Quantum systems, and Quantum Information Theory. Listed
below are a few sub-areas in which I have spent my time till now:
• Quantum Thermodynamics.
• Open Quantum System : Markovian & non-Markovian Dynamics, Collision models
• Quantum Thermal Devices : Quantum Engine, Quantum Refrigerator, Quantum Battery,
Quantum Transistor
• Resource Theory : Catalytic Resource theory.
• Different Quantum and Classical Correlations with Decoherence.
• Quantum Metrology : Quantum Thermometry.
• Quantum Jarzynski Equality in Open Quantum Systems.
• Quantum Biology : Quantum Coherence and Entanglement in the Avian Compass: Avian
Magnetoreception through ”radical pair” Model, Biological Heat Engines.
• Simulation : Molecular Dynamics (Simulation).
• Soft Condensed Matter : Thermodynamics of bio-molecules.
