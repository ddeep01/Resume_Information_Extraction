-
Tentative Thesis Title: "Forest Dependence, Management and Ecological Identity: Exploring
Interlinkages in Western Indian Himalayan Region".
Thesis Supervisor: Dr Shyamasree Dasgupta

-
Outline of the work: My work explores the interlinkages between forest dependence, forest
management, and ecological identity in the Western Indian Himalayan Region (WIHR). In this context,
I am trying to understand the reasons and effects of the differences in stakeholders' perceptions.
Alongside, the work aims to examine the issue of community participation in Indian forest management.
