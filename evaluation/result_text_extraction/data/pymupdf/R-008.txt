Research Interests
My broader area of research is to work on the existence of primitive pair, the existence of r-primitive
k-normal pair and the existence of a finite arithmetic progression of r-primitive elements with first
term being k-normal, with prescribed traces and norms over finite fields. Further, numerical
calculations are carried in SageMath to provide some illustrations.
